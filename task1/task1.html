RRR makes history

Oscars 2023 full list of nominations was announced on Tuesday evening. SS Rajamouli's RRR has made history as first Telugu film to get Best Song nomination.
SS Rajamouli's fictional pre-Independence epic has made history at the 95th Academy Awards. The Telugu blockbuster managed a nomination in the Best Song category.
